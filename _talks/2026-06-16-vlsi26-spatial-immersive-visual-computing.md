The Symposium on VLSI Technology and Circuits (VLSI) is one of the world's premier conferences, bringing together researchers from both academia and industry to present the latest advances spanning device technology, circuits, and systems. "NOVA: A 5.3mJ/frame Fully Integrated Neural Video SoC with Content-Adaptive Architecture for 4K Real-time Encoding/Decoding and Processing" is presented at the Accelerators for Spatial and Immersive Visual Computing session.
